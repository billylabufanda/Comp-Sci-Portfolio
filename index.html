Summit Shasta:

Summit Shasta is a revolutionary new school focused on making a diverse student body ready for college and have the core cognitive abilities required to succeed. Our electives, or Expeditions, are every six weeks for two weeks, instead of having the elective be one of your daily classes. This allows for more immersion into your elective.
