How My Bot Worked

For the last interval of Expeditions, our class coded fighting bots. We had a server where these bots would duel, competing for who could collect the most gold. The maps were randomly generated, with arbitrary numbers of mines, which were how you collected resources, taverns for healing, and obstacles to make it harder to maneuver. It was turn based, every turn allowing for moving, collecting a mine, or going to a tavern. You lose 1 of your 100 health every turn, getting attacked takes 20 health every hit, and a tavern heals 50 health in exchange for 2 gold. Mines give you 1 gold per turn.
