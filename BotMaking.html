Fighting Bots

For the last interval of Expeditions, our class coded fighting bots. Once in the arena, we had no control over what they would do. It was all up to what we coded.

The Arena

The maps were randomly generated, with arbitrary numbers of mines, taverns, and obstacles. You fight 3 other The mines collect one gold per turn, taverns take 2 gold but give you 50 health (you start with 100), and obstacles do nothing but make it harder to navigate the map. Players can take 20 points, and can own mines that take 20 health to capture.
